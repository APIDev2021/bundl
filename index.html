Welcome to 'Devware' the single stop, all in one platform for crypto development. Go from an outside to inside the loop dev, with easy to use AI powered tools, allowing you to both: Deploy unique contracts, bundle unlimited supply. Our ai powered contract creator 'Dev Buddy' offers unique contract functions and operates within a slick AI chat dapp format. Our true bundler 'Bundl Buddy' is the fastest of its kind, able to actually deploy and buy unlimited supply within the same block. Seamlessly move between these two tools and master the art of running your own project. With a person to person discord our team are available at anytime to answer your questions and through our 'Dev Academy' we'll teach you to become a successful dev, with achievement levels based on use of 'Devware' tools.

The $DVWR token operates as a gate for 'Bundl Buddy' and has revenue share capability for future utility development. Never before has a project so transparently put the trade secrets of crypto development into your hands.

Dev Buddy
The Devware 'Dev Buddy' is a first of its kind AI powered chat bot, allowing the user to create complex solidity codes and launch unique tokens on multiple chains. Simply explain to the bot the type of contract you need, customise the name, symbol, token supply and tax along with extra unique features:
The ability to have a unique token address (Example: beginning in 0x69 or 0x420)
The ability to add emojis to a ticker or name
The ability to add a burn percentage per transaction
The ability to add a preloaded amount to any CA
